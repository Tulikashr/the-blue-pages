About Me

Welcome to The Blue Pages! I'm Tulika Sharma, the creator and sole author of this blog. I started this space as a personal outlet to share my thoughts and passions across a variety of topics that resonate with me.

My Interests

My interests are quite diverse, which is why you'll find a range of categories here:

  • Cricket (Cerulean): As a fervent follower of the sport, I love to delve into match analyses, player performances, historical moments, and the evolving strategies of cricket.
  • Philosophy (Verglas): I enjoy exploring philosophical concepts, their relevance in modern life, and how different schools of thought can offer new perspectives on everyday challenges.
  • Society & Culture (Lapis): This section is dedicated to observations and discussions on current societal trends, cultural phenomena, and their impact on our lives.
  • Books & Literature (Indigo): A lifelong reader, I share my reviews, interpretations, and recommendations for both classic and contemporary works of literature.
  • Movies & TV (Midnight): From cinematic masterpieces to binge-worthy series, I discuss storytelling, character development, and the art of visual narrative.

My Vision for The Blue Pages

My goal with The Blue Pages is to create a thoughtful and engaging space where I can express my ideas and hopefully spark interesting conversations. I believe in the power of sharing perspectives and learning from one another.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find something here that interests, entertains, or inspires you!
